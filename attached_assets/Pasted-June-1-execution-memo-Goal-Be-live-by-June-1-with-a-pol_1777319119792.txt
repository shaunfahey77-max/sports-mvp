June 1 execution memo

Goal
Be live by June 1 with a polished, trustworthy application and a model stack that is honest about what is Official, what is Model Watch, and what is Disabled.

Important:
“All leagues and all markets” for launch does NOT mean every market is Official.
It means every target league/market has an intentional launch state, and only proven markets are promoted to Official.

Priority order
1. Truth layer
2. Replay / validation
3. Controlled market recovery
4. Official promotion only where earned
5. Product polish and launch lock

Launch gates
A market can be Official only if it shows:
- positive mean CLV
- acceptable recent realized performance
- enough settled sample to matter
- no obvious rank/tier inversion
- no data-integrity issues
- stable publish behavior

If a market does not clear that bar by launch:
- Model Watch only
or
- Disabled

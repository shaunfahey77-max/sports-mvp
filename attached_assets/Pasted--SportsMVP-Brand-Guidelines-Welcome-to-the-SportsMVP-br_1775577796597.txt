# SportsMVP Brand Guidelines

Welcome to the SportsMVP brand guidelines. This document serves as a comprehensive guide to maintaining the integrity and consistency of the SportsMVP brand across all platforms and communications.

## Logo

The SportsMVP logo is the cornerstone of our brand identity. It should be used consistently to ensure brand recognition.

- **Primary Logo**: The primary logo should be used on all official communications and is best presented with our primary color palette.
- **Clear Space**: Ensure there is sufficient space around the logo to maintain its impact and legibility. The minimum clear space should be equal to the height of the "S" in SportsMVP.
- **Minimum Size**: To ensure legibility, the logo should never be reproduced smaller than 1 inch in width for print or 72 pixels for digital formats.

## Color Palette

The SportsMVP color palette is designed to convey energy, passion, and reliability.

- **Primary Colors**:
  - **MVP Blue**: #0033A0
  - **Victory Red**: #D32F2F
- **Secondary Colors**:
  - **Action Green**: #388E3C
  - **Highlight Yellow**: #FFC107
- **Neutral Colors**:
  - **Slate Gray**: #424242
  - **Clean White**: #FFFFFF

## Typography

Consistent typography is crucial for brand recognition. The following fonts have been selected to reflect the dynamic and energetic nature of SportsMVP.

- **Primary Typeface**: *Montserrat*
  - Use for headings and prominent text.
- **Secondary Typeface**: *Roboto*
  - Use for body copy and supplementary text.

### Typography Guidelines

- **Headings**: Bold, size 24-36pt
- **Subheadings**: Bold, size 18-24pt
- **Body Text**: Regular, size 12-16pt

## Recommended Stock Photography

Photography used in SportsMVP materials should capture the essence of sportsmanship, energy, and empowerment. Consider the following guidelines:

- **Action Shots**: Images should depict athletes in motion, showcasing strength and determination.
- **Diversity**: Ensure a diverse representation of athletes in terms of gender, ethnicity, and sports disciplines.
- **Vibrant and Dynamic**: Photos should be high-resolution, vibrant, and convey a sense of movement and excitement.

## Usage Examples

### Do's

- Use the primary logo on white or neutral backgrounds.
- Maintain the integrity of the logo by using approved color palettes.
- Employ the primary and secondary typefaces in all branded materials.

### Don'ts

- Do not alter the logo colors outside the approved palette.
- Never stretch or distort the logo.
- Avoid cluttering the space around the logo with text or other graphics.

By adhering to these guidelines, we ensure that the SportsMVP brand remains strong, recognizable, and consistent across all platforms. Thank you for being a part of our team and helping to uphold the SportsMVP identity.

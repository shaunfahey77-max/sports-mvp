# SportsMVP Competitive Analysis
**Date:** April 2026  
**Question:** Can we charge $19.99/month?

---

## Market Landscape

The sports betting analytics subscription market breaks into three segments:
1. **Data/Odds Tools** — line movement, public money %, injury reports
2. **Expert Pick Services** — human handicappers selling picks per-game or via subscription  
3. **Model-Driven Prediction Services** — algorithmic/AI picks with stated methodology

SportsMVP competes in segment 3, which commands the strongest willingness to pay among sharp/semi-sharp bettors.

---

## Competitor Pricing & Features

### 1. The Action Network Pro — $19.99/month ($8.33/mo annual)
**URL:** actionnetwork.com  
**Strengths:**
- Massive brand recognition; most-visited betting media site
- Live odds from 50+ sportsbooks, line movement tracking
- Expert picks with real handicapper names (Stuckey, Koerner, etc.)
- Public betting % and sharp money tracking
- Bet tracking + community features

**Weaknesses:**
- Expert picks are opinion-based, not model-driven
- No stated calibration, EV calculations, or CLV tracking
- No transparent methodology; "trust the expert" model
- Broad coverage (many sports) but shallow on each

**Verdict:** Same price point as our target. We offer *more transparency* and a *rigorous ML pipeline* — strong differentiation.

---

### 2. BetQL — $12.99–$54.95/week (approx. $20–$60/month)
**URL:** betql.co  
**Strengths:**
- Simulates each game 10,000 times with ML model
- 5-star value rating system
- Line movement updated every 5 minutes
- Sharp action reports, player props tool
- 14 sports leagues covered

**Weaknesses:**
- Opaque model — no stated calibration methodology or EV formula
- Expensive if paying per-sport ($12.99+/week = $50+/month)
- All-sport annual at ~$300/year ($25/month) is the best value
- Weekly pricing model is confusing and off-putting

**Verdict:** Premium pricing vs. us. We win on transparency (published EV formula, Brier scores, CLV hit rate) and simplicity of pricing.

---

### 3. Dimers Pro — $24.99/month ($16.67/mo annual)
**URL:** dimers.com  
**Strengths:**
- AI chatbot (Dimebot) for personalized pick research
- Daily +EV bets, player projections, 22,000+ games/year
- Refreshes models with injuries, weather, news daily
- Private Discord community, live webinars, one-on-one sessions
- Mobile app with parlay picker and cheat sheets

**Weaknesses:**
- $24.99/month (monthly) is above our target price
- Very broad coverage dilutes focus — you can't be great at everything
- Less focus on NBA/NHL depth vs. our specialized models
- No published calibration scores or Brier score transparency

**Verdict:** More expensive than us (monthly). We're sharper on model transparency and NBA/NHL focus.

---

### 4. Sharp Football Analysis / Sports Insights — $29.99/month
**URL:** sportsbettingcommunity.com  
**Strengths:**
- Historical backtesting ("Bet Labs") with 15+ years of data
- Strong NFL focus with proprietary power ratings
- Sharp money tracking and steam move alerts

**Weaknesses:**
- NFL-centric; NBA/NHL coverage is secondary
- Dated interface and UX compared to modern apps
- No real-time model scoring or calibration metrics

**Verdict:** More expensive, narrower focus. We win on UX, NBA/NHL depth, and modern ML pipeline.

---

### 5. VSiN (Vegas Stats & Information Network) — $9.99–$29.99/month
**URL:** vsin.com  
**Strengths:**
- Insider information from Vegas-based handicappers
- Podcast content, radio show
- Line movement and sharp action data

**Weaknesses:**
- More media/content play than pure prediction tool
- Picks are opinion-based, not model-driven
- Variable quality — depends on which handicapper

**Verdict:** Different product category (media + picks vs. pure ML prediction). We win on systematic methodology.

## Pricing Recommendation

### $19.99/month is the RIGHT price. Here's why:

**It positions us alongside Action Network Pro** — the most trusted name in the space — while offering significantly more transparency and a more rigorous scientific methodology. Our transparent Brier scores, EV calculations, and CLV tracking are features *no major competitor currently offers publicly*.

**It undercuts Dimers Pro by $5/month** — the closest direct AI/ML competitor — while targeting a more focused niche (NBA + NHL, not 22,000 games across every sport).

**Sharp bettors will pay a premium for transparency.** A bettor making $50+ bets regularly will pay $19.99/month without hesitation if the model demonstrates genuine calibration and positive CLV.

### Suggested Tier Structure:

| Tier | Price | Features |
|---|---|---|
| **Free** | $0 | Today's top pick of the day (1 per day), no metrics |
| **MVP** | $19.99/mo or $149/yr ($12.50/mo) | All picks (A/B/C tiers), full metrics, history, performance dashboard |
| **MVP Pro** | $39.99/mo or $299/yr ($24.92/mo) | Everything in MVP + best-line alerts, email notifications, API access |

### Key Differentiators to Lead With:
1. **"We show our math"** — EV formula, calibrated probabilities, Brier score
2. **"CLV tracked so you know if you're getting good lines"** — no one else prominently features this
3. **"Automated — no human picks, no bias"** — picks update every 10 minutes with real odds
4. **"Depth over breadth"** — NBA + NHL with rigorous models, not 22,000 shallow predictions
5. **"Performance verified"** — 55.8% win rate, +22.9% ROI, +84.5 units tracked since launch

## Key Risk: Mobile App Gap

All top competitors have native mobile apps. A web-only experience is a disadvantage for bettors checking picks at the sportsbook. **Priority post-MVP: responsive mobile web at minimum, React Native app as growth milestone.**
